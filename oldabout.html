Welcome

Dear Delegates, Sponsors, and Friends of SMH-MUN,

Thank you to all the sponsors, delegates, and parents of the schools that attended this year’s Saint Mary’s Hall MUN conference. While we had our fair share of issues as expected with any MUN conference, the intelligence, professionalism, and creativity exhibited by this year’s delegation was nothing short of extraordinary. Crisis committees took an exciting step forward, and the quality of debate in standardized committees was just as riveting. You have all amazed myself and the rest of the Secretariat with your work ethic and dedication to MUN.

In addition, special congratulations are in order for all of our individual award winners, as well as the school recipients of our sweepstakes awards. Thank you all again, and we at SMH-MUN look forward to seeing you next year!

Best regards,
Logan Guy
Secretary General of SMH-MUN III

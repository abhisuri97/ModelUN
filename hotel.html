The official SMH-MUN III conference hotel is Hyatt Place San Antonio Airport/Quarry Market. Beginning Friday, March 20, the Hyatt Place will offer SMH-MUN III participants, a discounted night rate of $105.00 USD plus tax* for standard king (1-2 people) or double (up to 4 people). The hotel is conveniently located just 6 miles from Saint Mary’s Hall and 7 miles from downtown, including the Riverwalk. Although optional, we suggest that out of town delegations arrive a day early. Please note, optional training sessions for schools with novice delegates will be provided on the afternoon of March 20 and the morning of March 21.

SMH-MUN III has a block of rooms available from now through Friday, March 6 only. All other requests will be based on availability. You can call Ms. Sarah Embry at (210) 930-2333 ext. 154 and mention “Model United Nations Conference,” or reserve online using the code: G-MUNC and proceed to enter the date (March 20) and press search. This will direct you to a booking page showing the group’s name and rooms at our special rate.

If you will be arriving prior to March 20 and/ or departing after March 21, please contact Ms. Sarah Embry directly, in order to receive the discounted rate, as the online reservation is only available for the contracted date – March 20.

SMH-MUN III does not provide reimbursement for any prorated fees that may be charged by the Hyatt Place upon arrival or departure from the hotel. Breakfast will be offered as well as shuttle transportation to and from the hotel and the conference, provided that a minimum 10 rooms are reserved.

*Call hotel regarding school tax exemption.

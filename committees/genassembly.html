GENERAL ASSEMBLY

Preparing the Global Economy for a reduction in oil supply



Download .PDF

The central MUN governing body of our conference will be examining how passing Peak Oil (the most oil we will ever have available at one time) will affect various aspects of developed and developing nations. Delegates should consider economic, political, and social ramifications of the decline in world oil supply, and should work to actively reform the energy landscape in order compensate for the void left by oil.

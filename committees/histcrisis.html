HISTORICAL CRISIS

Warring States Period in China 475-221 B.C.



Download .PDF

Delegates will play the role of one of the leaders of one of the seven warring states that formed following the Spring and Autumn period of the Eastern Zhou dynasty. In order to successfully bring the Warring States period to a close with their state consolidating a new empire, delegates will have to form effective alliances, issuing action orders and troop movements that best advance the cause of their state.
